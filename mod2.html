    Description

    About

    Our hackathon idea addresses challenges faced by marginalized communities, such as unfair treatment, dependence, and limited opportunities. The problem we aim to solve is the lack of a dedicated platform that caters to their needs.

    Approach

    Our solution is a social platform connecting marginalized individuals, fostering networks, free skills development, and providing information on benefits, scholarships, and job opportunities. Here, people can offer skills, jobs, and organize public meetings on specific topics of interest,respecting Indian constitutional principles.

    Tech side

    We plan to implement the platform using a modern technology stack—HTML/CSS, Python, Django—for a user-friendly and scalable solution.
